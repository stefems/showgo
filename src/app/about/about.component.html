ShowGo

Once you login into ShowGo with facebook you'll be able to browse through a feed of concerts happening in Denver, Colorado. Click the play button to hear what the bands sound like, hit the join button to keep track of the shows you're interested in, and use the menu button at the top left to add useful filters. Tell your friends about our site! Once they login you'll get an alert so that you can follow what shows they're going to. You're able to send show invites to friends too! ShowGo has plans in the future for implementing a genre filter and venue filter for the events feed, so stay tuned! Feel free to reach out to Stefan at stefan.kowal@showgo.io if you have questions, feedback, or want to get involved.
